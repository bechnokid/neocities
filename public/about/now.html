Who am I?

Software developer and semi-professional artist.

By day, I am a full-stack software developer, which means that I work on both the backend (the coding) and the frontend (UI) of the application that I'm assigned to. It's fun and I'm learning new things every day.

By night (and weekends), I am an artist who enjoys drawing but am usually too tired to get anything done that night, so I usually end up playing video games lol. But that's ok. I'm in no rush to draw/post anything for my social media profiles.

I have done a total of three small gigs for an animation project, two of which include designing t-shirts. I think that's a pretty big accomplishment!
