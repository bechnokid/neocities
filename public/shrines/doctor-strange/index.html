Who is Doctor Strange?

Doctor Stephen Strange is a Marvel superhero and a Master of the Mystic Arts who first appeared in Strange Tales #110 in July 1963.

Yes, he is an actual doctor!

He was a skilled and arrogant neurosurgeon until a horrific car accident left him with permanent nerve damage in his hands, promptly ending his career. He then travels to Tibet (Nepal in the MCU) to seek a cure that will heal his hands, and meets a wise sorcerer called the Ancient One who teaches him magic.

Although Stephen can never return to his previous job as a surgeon, he instead finds a new purpose as a Master of the Mystic Arts, and uses his newfound magical powers to protect the world from mystical threats.

History with Doctor Strange

I was actually never interested in Doctor Strange until May 2022, when I saw Doctor Strange in the Multiverse of Madness in theaters. I actually only watched it because I just happened to finish Wandavision and I thought, "Oh hey! I might not actually be confused in this one!" I still got confused, but I enjoyed the story and Stephen's character.

However, Doctor Strange did not become a primary interest until I embarked on a Disney Magic Cruise at the end of August 2022, where I saw the same film five times in one of the cruises's numerous theaters as well as the 2016 film in the room I stayed in. After returning home, I completed my personal goal of watching every MCU film he appeared in.

I guess you can say I now have a...Strange addiction. ;)
