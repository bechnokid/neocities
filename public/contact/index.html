Contact Me

Some Reasons to Contact Me

  • There's something broken or a bug on my site.
  • You need help with coding that not even W3Schools can help with.
    • You can use Code Images (preferred) or take a screenshot of your code.
  • We have something in common! I'd love to talk about it!
  • Virtual pet tips. This includes vintage Tamagotchi and most Digital Monsters devices.
  • Specific art questions, such as what brushes I used, how I achived certain effects/filters, etc.
    • If your first question is something along the lines of, "How can I get better at art?" My only answer is to reflect within yourself and find what it is about your art that you want to get better at, whether it's anatomy, backgrounds, animals, etc. Then, see if you can find references online. If you want to avoid AI-generated art, you can add "-ai" after your prompt or adjust the search results to only return images before 2021-2022.

Important Notes

  • I am an adult in her 30s. While I would prefer talking and making friends with people around my age, I don't mind talking to younger people if it's in a semi-professional setting like talking to a teacher!
  • Unless we're close, please don't come into my inbox to rant or vent without asking first!
  • I prefer email more than instant messaging in apps such as Discord, WhatsApp, etc.
  • Talking about anything remotely NSFW is out of the question. I'd rather you not tell me things you wouldn't tell your grandparents.
  • Although English is my first language, I grew up with parents with English as their second/third language, so there's a lot of slang that I don't know but am willing to learn!
