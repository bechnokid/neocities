[SSBU AU] Distraction, Pg. 2

(Dec. 2022) This was a comic I did in response to an anon ask pointing out how Ganon's nose looks like a beak (which I completely agree and that's what I love about his nose! :3). I wanted to establish how these characters contribute to the relationship. Ganondorf obviously provides physical strength and can protect the otherwise weaker Linebeck when needed. Linebeck is an expert traveler and can provide emotional support in his own silly way. :D
