New to Feeds?

You can learn more about feeds, which newsreaders to install, and how to subscribe to your favorite webmasters' feeds over at About Feeds.

Heather from Yogo Magpie also goes into great detail about them, so be sure to check out her article, too! There's also a great template you can use.

I personally use FreshRSS (browser) and Feedbro (Chrome/Firefox/Edge extension).

Want to Make Your Own?

Here are a couple of my favorite resources on making your own feeds:

  • Style your RSS feed by Darek Kay - shares how to make your RSS pretty and easy for users to read
  • RSS feeds tutorial by Des - also shows how to display RSS feeds on HTML! Super helpful and adorable to boot!
