Microblog

Thoughts and ramblings that are otherwise too small to make into a blog post!

January 9th, 2025 | 🎉

Happy belated new year! MAGfest is just in the horizon, and I'm always excited! I'm actually glad it's going to be later in the month, so hopefully it'll warm up if just for a little bit lol.

December 9th, 2024 | 😍

Square-Enix uploaded a 1-hour version of several covers and arrangements of "Battle at the Big Bridge"! I'm so happy!! "May everyone stay focused alongside Gilgamesh". I sure will!! 😊

June 23rd, 2024 | 😮‍💨

It helps to have a sister who's a psychiatrist lol. She gave me a diagnosis and told me that I'm on the autism spectrum. I'm honestly not surprised, but it's honestly a relief to hear this from a medical professional who is also neurodivergent (she has ADHD). While a lot of things suddenly make sense, things are pretty much going to stay the same, and I'm content with that.

February 23rd, 2024 | 😍

My birthday was on the 10th, Valentine's Day has come and gone, and Katsucon was on the weekend on the 16th! It's been a pretty eventful month for me, especially since my wife and I both got super into the Vital Bracelet BE and modding both the BE and DIM cards for our Digimon! I swear we're not obsessed lol.

February 6th, 2024 | 😅

I swear I'm alive! Things have just been really busy, but I hope everyone had a good holiday! Slowly chugging away at 11ty and I'm not even sure if it'll be worth it in the end! Oh well, it's been fun to work on when I have time. :3
