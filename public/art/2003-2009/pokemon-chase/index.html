Pokemon Chase

<< Index >>
From August 2007.
My entry for the awesome Pokecon contest. Ok, ok, I have to admit, I was quite bored and I felt guilty for not drawing Charlie the Tentacool often so this is to make it up.

In here, an Aipom just stole Zaphk's character Korin's bag. Charlie was walking along and saw how devastated Korin looked after his bag was stolen. After all, if your bag had all of your pokeballs and stuff in there, you would be devastated if it was stolen, too. xD So Charlie soon became a hero and went after that Aipom.

Ok, so this is the first time that I've done a totally realistic, yet not-so-realistic background. It was quite fun, really. xD Especially the trees. Also, this is the first time that I have painted water.

Anyway, I hope you like this, and critique is welcome. 8D
Man!! I should go back to doing backgrounds. Idk how younger me was able to do it so well! Maybe because she was more focused on having fun rather than being scared that she'll mess up, which is how I usually think today lol
Transcript

Chrlie (Tentacool): You there! Return that bag to Trainer Korin this instant!

??? (Aipom): You gotta go faster than that if you want this, chump!
