At The Bus Stop

<< Index >>
From February 2008.
Jack and Jill meet Mr. Happy for the first time. EPIC, MUCH?

Haha, this was pretty much drawn after I saw the prize that Pumpkin-Girl gave me. She drew the crazy duo with modern clothes, which I found so spectacular that I just HAD to draw them. Seriously, the outfits that she gave them were so adorable! 8DDD

Plus, I wanted to see how their first encounter with Mr. Happy would be when he just finished his job. This was another excuse of drawing Mr. Happy because of the wonderful mug that Zody gave to him. xDDDD
Fun fact about this piece! I did the lineart in Adobe Flash! Ah...the days where lines in Flash was one of the only ways to have clean and crisp lines lol.
