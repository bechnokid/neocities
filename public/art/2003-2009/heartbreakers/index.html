Heartbreakers

<< Index >>
From September 2008.
I'm sure that you guys have seen Rosalivia around in my gallery. If not, then here she is again. I swear, she is one of the only OC's that has gone through the most changes considering her design. I was never satisfied with her outfit until now. She has gone through drastic changes as you can tell. Sometimes I can't believe that this is her from 2006....(link) xDD Man, oh, man.

And Etahis here is a total revamp from this in 2005: (link) That was basically a Nights rip-off and originally a girl LOL. I didn't want this...thing to be a dead character, so I thought I could revamp it into a much more useful person. Notice how I still kept his old colors and that his name is simply an anagram of his old name, Shiate.

Now, I'd like to tell these guys' descriptions.

Rosalivia thinks that love is always corrupt and causes ruin upon everything. In becoming banished from her former world and later destroying it, she went on a rampage to make sure she gets what she wants. So she plans to create an empire void of all love by conquering worlds one by one using her almighty power. She already has an army of creatures born from nightmares and broken hearts assisting her in making sure everything goes according to plan.

Etahis is a creature of an unknown species. He was also banished from his world for his diabolical schemes to conquer it. This exile almost brought upon his death until Rosalivia found him. Rosalivia noticed his high intellect and power, and so she decided to recruit him as her right-hand man. A rather emotionless being, Etahis takes his work seriously and is always making sure everything goes as planned, even if it means going as far as killing anyone who stands in his leader's way.

I don't feel like revealing too much about them, yet. Perhaps when I make some ref sheets for them.
It's hilarious that Rosa's design seems to change every other year while Etahis has remained the same lmao.
