Too Much Water, 7.8

<< Index >>
From February 2015.
The second in the series of the Mystery Skulls Gang and their official (more or less) Pokemon teams, with Vivi this time around! I've been working on this for a long time due to lack of inspiration at the time, as it is such a fickle thing. >:I But I had a lot of fun working on this, nonetheless! I'm super happy with how the water and clouds turned out. And although you cannot really tell, that is indeed a shiny Froslass in Vivi's team!
I'm really bummed that I never finished this series! After the crash, I lost Ben's messages for the rest of the cast's Pokemon teams! I'll have to work up the courage to ask him again one of these days!
